How Resident Evil Revolutionized Gaming

Resident Evil helped define the survival horror genre by focusing on tension, fear, and limited resources. Instead of constant action, the game required players to think strategically and manage what they had.

The cinematic camera angles and immersive atmosphere made players feel like they were inside a horror movie. This design approach influenced many future games and demonstrated that video games could create powerful emotional experiences.
