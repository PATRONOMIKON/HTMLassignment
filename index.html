Introduction to Resident Evil

The Story of Resident Evil

Resident Evil is a survival horror video game series created by Capcom that began in 1996. The first game is set in a mansion near Raccoon City, where players control special agents investigating mysterious murders.

Inside the mansion, players discover that a company called Umbrella Corporation created a deadly virus that turns people into zombies. The story focuses on survival, mystery, and escaping while uncovering the truth behind the outbreak.

Main Features of the Series

  • Survival horror gameplay
  • Limited ammo and resources
  • Puzzle solving
  • Strong storytelling
